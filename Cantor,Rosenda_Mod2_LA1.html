Apples are nutritious fruits

Apples are round fruits with a red or green skin and a whitish inside. Here are some health benefits of apples in the human body:

  • Helps boost the immune system
  • Can aid digestion
  • It may lower high cholesterol and blood pressure

With so many health benefits it offers, it truly keeps the doctor away if you eat it every day.
